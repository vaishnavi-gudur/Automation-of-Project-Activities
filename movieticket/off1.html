ICICI Bank Credit Card Rs.100 Offer

ABOUT THE OFFER

Get Rs 100 off on your total ticket cost booked with your ICICI Bank Credit Card . Offer only on select ICICI Bank Credit Cards (refer eligible cards section under terms and conditions for details of the offer )

HOW TO AVAIL OFFER

Go through the regular ticketing flow for selecting the movie, cinema and show of your choice. Confirm your details by providing your email id & mobile number as this information will be used for confirming your transaction. Proceed to the secure payment page, enter your 16 digit ICICI bank Credit Card number in the box, name on the card, expiry and CVV card details. To avail offer, check 'Avail Discounts & Offers' at the bottom left side of the page and ensure that you are logged in, if not already logged- in then please complete the prompt. Select 'Offers', select ICICI Bank Rs.100 off Offer from the list and click on the 'Apply Offer' button Congratulations! You would have successfully availed your offer.
