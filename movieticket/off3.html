MasterCard Debit Card Offers

ABOUT THE OFFER

First-time Mastercard Debit Card users can avail of INR 50 off on movie tickets.

HOW TO AVAIL OFFER

Go through the regular ticketing flow for selecting the movie, cinema and show of your choice. Confirm your details by providing your email id & mobile number as this information will be used for confirming your transaction. Proceed to the secure payment page, enter your 16 digit SBI Signature Credit Card number in the box, name on the card, expiry and CVV card details. To avail offer, check 'Avail Discounts & Offers' at the bottom left side of the page. Select 'Offers', select "SBI Signature Credit Card Rs.500 off Offer" from the list and click on the 'Apply Offer' button. Congratulations! You would have successfully availed your offer.

TERMS & CONDITIONS

The customer needs to purchase minimum 2 movie tickets to avail the offer. The number of tickets discounted under this offer are limited and customers can avail this offer on a First Come First Serve basis every day of the week. It is also subject to availability at the cinema. Offer is applicable for ticket bookings relating to all cinemas in India where tickets can be booked at www.bookmyshow.com. In case you have applied for the discount but the transaction doesn't go through for some reason, kindly wait for 20 minutes before trying to avail the discount again.
