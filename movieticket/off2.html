ABOUT THE OFFER

Get 2 free tickets per month or Rs.500 off whichever is less.
Offer Valid on purchase of minimum 2 tickets.

HOW TO AVAIL OFFER

Go through the regular ticketing flow for selecting the movie, cinema and show of your choice. Confirm your details by providing your email id & mobile number as this information will be used for confirming your transaction. Proceed to the secure payment page, enter your 16 digit SBI Signature Credit Card number in the box, name on the card, expiry and CVV card details. To avail offer, check 'Avail Discounts & Offers' at the bottom left side of the page. Select 'Offers', select "SBI Signature Credit Card Rs.500 off Offer" from the list and click on the 'Apply Offer' button. Congratulations! You would have successfully availed your offer.

TERMS & CONDITIONS

This offer is valid only on SBI Signature credit card. Customers can avail this offer on a purchase of minimum of 2 tickets per transaction. There is no cap on the maximum or minimum price per ticket, however the maximum discount per ticket will Rs.250/- only. The customer can get 2 free movie tickets or Rs 500 off, whichever is less per transaction. The SBI Signature Credit card holder is eligible for only one discounted transaction per month.
